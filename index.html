ABOUT US


Welcome to CHAI SHAI ! We're thrilled to share our love of coffee, delicious treats, and cozy atmosphere with you. Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or a place to relax and unwind, our doors are always open. At CHAI SHAI, we take pride in roasting our coffee beans to perfection, ensuring every cup is rich, fresh, and full of flavor. Our menu also features locally sourced ingredients and a variety of handcrafted drinks, catering to all taste preferences—from classic espressos to inventive lattes and seasonal specials. Our cafe is designed to be a warm, inviting space where you can relax, meet friends, or find inspiration. With cozy seating, ambient music, and free Wi-Fi, we’ve created the perfect setting for both productivity and relaxation.
